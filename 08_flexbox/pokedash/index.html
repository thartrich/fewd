Gyms

Violet City

Led by Falkner

This gym is known to specialize in Flying type pokemon. Beware their aerial attacks. Be sure to bring your own thunder to finish them off.

Goldenrod

Led by Whitney

The pokemon here may be normal, but they are by no means ordinary. You will face off against several high-health pokemon that sport a variety of unpredictable moves. You'd best think hard about your party if you want a fighting chance.

Cianwood City

Led by Chuck

Cianwood City is a gym of martial arts. The pokemon here are trained and ruthless. Prepare to face karate chops, mach punches, and spinning kicks. You'll have to play mindgames with your opponent's psyche in order to win!

Mahogany Town

Led by Pryce

Mahogany Town may just be the coolest gym in all of Johto. I hope you packed warm jacket, because there's nothing but ice pokemon in here. You'll have to bring the heat in order to light a fire under your opponent's seat.
